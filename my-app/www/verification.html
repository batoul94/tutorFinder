Verification Process

Each tutor needs to create a profile before they can use the system. The profile should show off their skills, level of experience, availability, photo of each tutor is required for the profile, however to increase the security of the app and the safety of the children an external verification will be carried out before the registration process is completed.

Meet with our staff

1. An email confirming the date, time and location of the meeting will be sent to your email address (to change the date, send an email back to our staff stating your availability for the verification)
2. Bring your passport, student card, Nino and DBS certificate ( All documents must be original documents, not photocopies)
3. If the external verification was successful you can log in and edit your profile
